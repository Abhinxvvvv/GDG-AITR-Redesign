About GDG AITR

Empowering Students Through Technology

Google Developer Groups AITR is a vibrant community of students passionate about technology and innovation. We organize workshops, hackathons, and tech talks to help students explore Google technologies including Android, Web Development, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing.

Our mission is to create a collaborative environment where students can learn, share knowledge, and build amazing projects together. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, GDG AITR welcomes everyone to join our journey of continuous learning.

We believe in the power of community-driven learning and aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements through hands-on workshops and real-world projects.
